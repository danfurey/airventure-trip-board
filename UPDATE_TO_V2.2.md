# Update to v2.2

This release removes the Decisions tab and moves all shared choices into the Schedule view.

## Behavior

- Tap an event in the timeline, then choose **Attend**, **Maybe**, or **Skip**.
- **Attend** is the active group selection and immediately filters conflicting timeline events.
- Demonstrations, flexible display windows, and Air Show events stay visible.
- Air Show events occupy the featured lanes at the top of the timeline and retain the Air Show color.
- The full schedule remains complete. Conflicting options are grayed but can be selected with **Override & attend**.
- Existing overrides remain active until one of the Attend selections is cleared.

No Firebase rules or environment-variable changes are required.
